Not many DJs can claim they grew up DJing, however Eric, known as Evido, is one of the few. From private events to clubs and festivals, Evido is a highly-respected DJ with a depth of music knowledge allowing him the flexibility to perform in front of any audience. Since relocating to Nashville, he has held five DJ residencies and has performed on a handful of festival lineups.

Evido has a lengthy history performing with, and opening for internationally touring music acts such as Morgan Page, Krewella, Cedric Gervais, Asher Roth, Paul van Dyk, and many more. He has DJed on the road for the hip hop duo Grits and 1GN. Evido has DJed for brands such as Red Bull, Camp No Counselors, Malibu Rum, Belvedere, and Devoted Creations.

With a passion for dancing as well as producing music, Evido specializes in mixing and weaving tracks together, captivating the energy in the room. His music has been supported on SiriusXM, iHeartRadio, and A State Of Trance. Evido also is an A&R manager for Cloudland Music, a niche dance music label that licenses music for radio shows such as Armin van Buuren’s A State Of Trance, Above & Beyond’s Group Therapy Radio, and many more.
